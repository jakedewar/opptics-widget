Privacy Policy for Opptics Chrome Extension

Last updated: 12/19/2024

Data Collection

Opptics does not collect any personal information. All text replacements and settings are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API.

Data Storage

We store:

  • Your text replacement mappings
  • Template preferences
  • Extension settings

All data is stored locally and is not transmitted to any external servers.

Permissions

Our extension requires certain permissions to function:

  • storage: To save your settings and mappings
  • activeTab: To modify text on the current page
  • contextMenus: For right-click functionality
